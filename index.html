We find your flat for you.

Our services:

We help you gain an overview.

Camden, Hackney, Westminster, Islington. London is a diverse metropolis and every one of its areas has a different flair and culture. Even if you have lived in the City for a while it is hard to maintain an overview. Besides your budget and desired flat size, schools and childcare availability, neighborhood safety, vibrancy, recreation facilities, distance to your workplace, available transportation, and overall flair all may need to be considered to find the flat that is right for you. We help you make up your mind which spot is best for you, taking into account all your individual circumstances.

We help you understand the market.

Your time is precious and there is no need for you to waste it on learning everything there is to know about London rentals just to find a flat once. We will simply tell you everything you need to know. We help you understand the specifics of London’s rental market and advise you on what price tag is adequate for a property.

We protect you from scammers.

Excitement about finding the perfect flat online quickly turns bitter when an alleged landlord asks you to start off your engagement by wiring large sums to Columbia. Over the last years the number of property scams in London have steadily increased. Many such scams specifically target foreigners moving to London who can’t view the flat prior to their arrival. We are your on-site team who make sure that every offer is legitimate.

We do the flat search for you.

Do you really want to spend your time-off on looking for flats online and calling agents and landlords – often just to find that the listing you responded to is out of date and your desired flat is already gone? We find all the listings that match your unique requirements and make sure they are available. You get all the info – summarized in one convenient file.

We do your viewings for you.

The best time reach agents and landlords for viewings is Mon-Fri 9am-6pm. If you happen to be unemployed that is. How often did you already have to forfeit your lunch break to meet a landlord? How often did you anxiously sit in a taxi caught in traffic after looking at a flat before work, arriving to late for a meeting? How often did you have to leave work early to make it to an evening appointment with an agent? Perhaps you already though about sacrificing your holidays to go flat hunting instead? Or are you maybe even moving to London from abroad and consider flying to London just to find a room to rent? Lunch-break viewings no more! We do your viewings for you.

At each flat we create a viewing video including a commentary or we stream the viewing live to your computer, tablet, or mobile device. Lay back and conveniently watch the live viewing or the recorded files from the office, from home, from abroad. With choices narrowed down like this you can choose the one or two flats you actually want to view yourself – or rent it sight unseen.

We help you with paperwork and payments.

Once you signed a rental contract, landlords and agents require documents and deposit payments, often in cash. We find that you really shouldn’t need to drive all the way to an agent’s office just to drop off a few papers. Therefore, we enable you to provide any documentation remotely. We can also handle your deposit payments so that you don’t have to walk around London with an envelope full of cash.
